Services We Offer

Structural Assessment

Inspection, Testing and Condition Evaluation of Existing Concrete and Steel Structures to Understand the Extent and Cause of Distress.

Repair Strategy and Technical Scope

Development of Repair Methods, Specifications, Quantity Planning and Execution-Ready Technical Documentation.

Structural Strengthening

Strengthening Design and Execution of CFRP, GFRP, Micro-Concrete Jacketing, and Polymer-Modified Mortar Systems.

Corrosion Protection

Corrosion diagnosis, Cathodic Protection System Design, Durability Improvement and Monitoring Support for Reinforced Concrete Structures.

Grouting and Crack Repair

PU, Epoxy and Acrylate-Based Grouting Systems for Leakage Control, Crack Repair and Structural Restoration.

Industrial Flooring and Waterproofing

PU, Epoxy and Specialised Flooring and Waterproofing Systems for Industrial and High-Performance Service Conditions.
